Ramen Recipe

Description

Ramen Noodles is a great option for college studnets due to the fact that most dorms in America do not have kitchens in them. Ramen is a full meal that comes pre packed and ready to make with either hot water and or a microwave

Ingredients

The only ingredients needed is the pre packaged Ramen Noodle brand packs.

Steps

  1. Open the package and remove the plastic contents inside
  2. If you are picky feel free to remove the vegetables before the next step.
  3. Fill up the cup with water right up to the fill line located in the cup.
  4. Place the cup of ramen and water in the microwave for 3 minutes on high.
  5. Let the freshly made ramen cool off before consumption to avoid burns.
